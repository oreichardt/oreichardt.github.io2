I have been on a lot of meaningful trips in my life and I am very grateful. A spot that will forever hold a big piece of my heart though is Daytona Beach, Florida. I spent a week each summer going to Daytona Beach for as long as I remember. It was always the highlight of my summer going there with my family and friends. We were able to spend time as a family together, back in the warm weather, and go out to eat at great restaurants!

Favorite parts of the this spot:
  • Floating in the lazy river
  • Boogie boarding on the beach
  • Going to arcades on the boardwalk
  • Eating at Aunt Catfish

Last year, I took a spring break trip here with friends. We had the BEST time. I was so happy to show my friends my favorite place.
